What to wear in Delhi summer heat

Updated 2026-04-06 · From Style.ai

Delhi summers punish heavy layers and reward breathable cuts—but office AC can flip you from sweltering to freezing in one elevator ride. Build looks around fabrics you will actually wear outdoors, with a layer strategy for indoors.

Morning commute vs. afternoon cabin

Plan for the hottest part of your trip first, then add a structured layer that reads professional under harsh indoor light. Photograph both sides of your wardrobe so AI suggestions stay honest.

Color that survives dust and harsh sun

Palettes that flatter your undertone still matter when haze and glare flatten contrast. Use previews to sanity-check how reads change between daylight and cool-white office bays.

Footwear and ground heat

Asphalt heat travels up. Prioritize pairs you can walk in; build outfits from shoes outward on brutal days.

FAQ

How do I dress for Delhi heat and office AC?

Layer intentionally: breathable base, one add-on for freezing conference rooms, and shoes chosen for the outdoor segment first.
