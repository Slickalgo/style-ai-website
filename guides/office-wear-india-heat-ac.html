Office wear for India’s heat and freezing AC

Updated 2026-04-06 · Editorial guide from Style AI

Metro office dressing is two climates: the walk or ride in, and the conference room that feels like winter. The fix is not a heavier wardrobe—it is layering discipline and shoes chosen for the outdoor segment first.

Anchor on shoes and base layers

If feet suffer on the commute, the whole day sours. Pick footwear you can move in, then let separates and palette follow.

One polished layer for the cold zone

A structured jacket, stole, or long-sleeve layer that reads intentional beats a random hoodie when clients appear.

Let your catalog reflect both worlds

Photograph summer-weight workwear and the indoor layer as separate items so Style AI can propose combinations that match your actual office thermostat war.
