Capsule wardrobe outfit ideas

Updated 2026-04-06 · Editorial guide from Style AI

Capsules fail when pieces do not share a color story or silhouette language. Style AI helps you see combinations you are underusing before you buy another almost-right tee.

Define the capsule scope

Pick a season or a lifestyle bucket—workweek, travel, weekend parent. Smaller scopes mean better suggestions from limited items.

Force multi-use winners

Jackets that only match one pair of pants are capsule leaks. The app surfaces high-leverage items by how often they complete viable outfits.

Let undertone guide accent colors

Two accent hues that flatter your skin beat five trendy colors that fight your complexion.

FAQ

How many pieces belong in a capsule?

Common ranges are 25–40 including shoes, but the right number is what you will honestly maintain in the app with photos.
