What to wear in NYC in spring

Updated 2026-04-06 · Editorial guide from Style AI

Spring in New York is a moving target: chilly mornings, warm afternoons, and rain without warning. The winning formula is layers you can strip or add without ruining the look.

Pack layers, not bulk

Light base layers, a mid layer with structure, and a packable shell beat one heavy coat for most April and May days. Style AI can combine owned pieces into day-to-night stacks.

Footwear for miles

Subway stairs and sidewalk miles punish the wrong shoe. Prioritize pairs you already trust; let the app suggest outfits around them before you buy new sneakers.

Color that works indoors and out

Cafés and galleries shift lighting fast. Skin-aware palettes help you stay vibrant in both gray daylight and warm interiors.
