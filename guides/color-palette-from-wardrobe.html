Color palette from your wardrobe

Updated 2026-04-06 · From Style.ai

Your closet already votes for certain hues. Style AI reads those signals alongside your skin tone so new buys extend what works—not fight it.

Photograph in honest light

Fluorescent break rooms and golden hour patios both skew color. Aim for natural, indirect daylight when cataloging so the app sees fabric truth.

Cluster what repeats

Neutrals, denim washes, and accent colors you buy again and again are your real palette. The app helps surface those clusters as guidance for the next purchase.

Cross-check with skin tone

A favorite sweater might love you—or only work with makeup. Undertone-aware analysis helps separate emotional attachment from optical harmony.

FAQ

Can I rebuild my palette after a closet purge?

Yes. Refresh photos after big edits so recommendations track what is left on the rack.
