Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Indian legal entity that operates the Style AI brand (“Style AI,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the “Service”). The Service helps you capture wardrobe items, receive outfit and color recommendations, and use artificial intelligence (“AI”) features that process information you provide.

You may request our legal name and registered office by email to hello@styleai.app. By using the Service, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

Important: This Policy is not legal advice. If you need guidance for your situation, consult a qualified professional.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the Service.

Account and contact data

  • Registration and login. We collect your email address, authentication tokens, and credentials managed through our identity provider (including password hashes we do not store in plain text). If you use social or single sign-on in the future, we collect the identifiers the provider shares with us.
  • Profile and preferences. We collect the name, date of birth, measurements, style preferences, skin-tone-related inputs, and other fields you enter during onboarding or settings.

Photos, wardrobe, and user content

  • Images you capture or upload. We store photos of you, your clothing, tags, and related media you submit through the camera or gallery flows, including derived thumbnails and metadata (for example, MIME type and file size).
  • Wardrobe and catalog data. We store item labels, categories, colors, ratings, feedback you submit, and conversation or chat content you send through in-product assistants.

Usage, device, and diagnostic data

  • Product telemetry. We log events such as sign-in, feature usage, API requests, error codes, and performance timings. On mobile we collect operating system version, app version, device model, locale, and crash reports.
  • Support communications. When you contact us, we retain the message, attachments, and notes needed to respond.

Technical data

  • Server logs. Our systems record IP addresses, request paths, user agents, timestamps, and security signals needed to operate and protect the Service.
  • Cookies and local storage. We use cookies, device storage, and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and measure site usage. See Section 8.

2. How we use information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide the Service. Authenticate you, store your wardrobe, run styling and recommendation features, and deliver notifications you enable.
  • Operate AI features. Send prompts, images, and contextual data to model providers to generate recommendations, answers, and visualizations you request.
  • Improve and secure the Service. Debug issues, monitor reliability, prevent abuse, and develop new features. We do not use your photos or chat content to train third-party foundation models except where a separate feature clearly states otherwise in writing and you opt in.
  • Communicate with you. Send transactional messages (verification, password reset, security alerts) and, where permitted, product updates or marketing according to your preferences.
  • Comply with law. Meet legal, regulatory, and tax obligations and respond to lawful requests.

3. Legal bases (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

Where GDPR or equivalent law applies, we rely on:

  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests — securing the Service, understanding aggregate usage, direct marketing to business contacts where compatible with your rights, and internal reporting.
  • Consent — optional cookies, certain marketing, and any processing we present as consent-based.
  • Legal obligation — retention and disclosure where the law requires it.

4. How we share information

We disclose information to the following categories of recipients:

  • Cloud and authentication providers. We use Supabase (Supabase, Inc.) and similar infrastructure for authentication, databases, file storage, and hosting in the regions we configure for production.
  • AI model providers. We send prompts, images, and related context to providers such as OpenAI to generate styling outputs. Those providers process data under their terms and our instructions as processors where applicable.
  • Analytics and diagnostics. We use first-party and, when enabled, third-party tools to measure usage and crashes. The public marketing site at styledesigner.co.in may use Google Analytics 4 for aggregated traffic, conversion funnels, and performance signals (including Core Web Vitals) when that feature is enabled and you accept optional analytics cookies in the on-site banner (where shown). Until you accept, we do not load GA4 measurement tags on that page. We list major third-party analytics SDKs in-app or on our website when they process personal data.
  • Payment processors. If we offer paid features, payment details are collected by our processor; we receive limited billing metadata.
  • Professional advisers and corporate transactions. Lawyers, auditors, insurers, and successors in a merger or acquisition, subject to confidentiality and legal requirements.
  • Authorities. When we believe in good faith that disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, or security.

We do not sell your personal information as defined under U.S. state privacy laws, and we do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising through “sale” or “sharing” mechanisms.

5. Data retention

  • Account and profile data are kept while your account is active and for up to seven years after closure where tax, fraud, or legal hold requirements apply.
  • Photos, wardrobe items, and chat content are retained until you delete them or delete your account, after which they are removed from active systems; backup copies typically expire within thirty-five days.
  • Logs and telemetry are retained for up to twelve months, except longer retention for security investigations or legal obligations.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing, to withdraw consent, to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise rights, email hello@styleai.app. We verify requests by confirming control of your account email where appropriate.

If you are in India, you may also contact our grievance channel at the same email with “Grievance” in the subject line for matters under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, including access, correction, erasure, consent withdrawal, and nomination rights where applicable.

7. Account and data deletion

You can delete individual photos or wardrobe entries inside the app where those controls exist. To delete your entire account, use the in-app account or security settings if available, or email hello@styleai.app from your registered address with “Account Deletion” in the subject line. We acknowledge deletion requests within three business days and complete deletion of data not subject to a legal retention exception within thirty days.

8. Cookies and tracking

We use strictly necessary cookies and storage for sign-in and security. Where required, we ask consent before non-essential analytics or marketing cookies on our website. The mobile app does not use web advertising cookies inside the app experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings; blocking necessary cookies may break parts of the Service.

9. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest where supported by our providers, access controls, and monitoring designed to protect personal data. No system is perfectly secure; use a strong password and protect your device.

10. International transfers

We operate from India and use service providers in India, the United States, the European Union, and other regions. When we transfer personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms where required.

11. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our Terms require users to be at least 18 in any event. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact hello@styleai.app and we will delete it promptly.

12. Automated processing and AI

Outputs are generated automatically and may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a given context. You should not rely on AI outputs alone for decisions with legal, medical, financial, or safety implications. We do not use your content to train third-party foundation models except where a feature explicitly states otherwise and you opt in.

13. United States (California and other states)

California residents may request the rights described in Section 6 and may ask once per calendar year about certain disclosures for direct marketing purposes. We do not discriminate for exercising privacy rights. Authorized agents may submit requests where state law allows.

14. Changes to this Policy

We update this Policy when our practices change. We post the new version on this page and update the effective date. For material changes, we provide notice by email or in-app message where the law requires.
