Privacy Policy

Ukti collects no information on this site. ukti.io is a static editorial surface — it stores no email, no account, no cookie beyond what is strictly necessary for the page to render. When you tap the App Store or Google Play link, you leave ukti.io and the destination store's own privacy terms govern from that point.

This policy describes how the Indian legal entity that operates the Ukti brand (Ukti) handles personal data across its services, including the forthcoming Ukti app. You may request Ukti's legal name and registered office by email to privacy@ukti.io. By using the services, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, do not use the services.

Important: This policy is not legal advice. If you need guidance for your situation, consult a qualified professional.

1. Information Ukti collects

On ukti.io, Ukti collects nothing beyond the operational telemetry described in Section 8. The categories below apply when you create an account in the Ukti app.

Account and contact data

  • Registration and login. Ukti collects your email address, authentication tokens, and credentials managed through an identity provider (including password hashes that are never stored in plain text). If you use social or single sign-on in the future, Ukti collects the identifiers the provider shares.
  • Profile and preferences. Ukti collects the name, date of birth, measurements, style preferences, and other fields you enter during onboarding or settings.

Photos, wardrobe, and user content

  • Images you capture or upload. Ukti stores photos of you, your clothing, tags, and related media you submit through the camera or gallery flows, including derived thumbnails and metadata (for example, MIME type and file size).
  • Wardrobe and catalog data. Ukti stores item labels, categories, colors, ratings, and feedback you submit.

Usage, device, and diagnostic data

  • Product telemetry. Ukti logs events such as sign-in, feature usage, API requests, error codes, and performance timings. On mobile, Ukti collects operating system version, app version, device model, locale, and crash reports.
  • Support communications. When you contact Ukti, the message, attachments, and notes required to respond are retained.

Technical data

  • Server logs. Ukti's systems record IP addresses, request paths, user agents, timestamps, and security signals needed to operate and protect the services.
  • Cookies and local storage. Ukti uses cookies, device storage, and similar technologies only as described in Section 8.

2. How Ukti uses information

  • Provide the services. Authenticate you, store your wardrobe, run styling features, and deliver notifications you enable.
  • Operate styling features. Send prompts, images, and contextual data to model providers to generate Ukti's daily call for you.
  • Improve and secure the services. Debug issues, monitor reliability, prevent abuse, and develop new features. Ukti does not use your photos or chat content to train third-party foundation models except where a separate feature clearly states otherwise in writing and you opt in.
  • Communicate with you. Send transactional messages (verification, password reset, security alerts) and, where permitted, product updates according to your preferences.
  • Comply with law. Meet legal, regulatory, and tax obligations and respond to lawful requests.

3. Legal bases (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

Where GDPR or equivalent law applies, Ukti relies on:

  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the services you request.
  • Legitimate interests — securing the services, understanding aggregate usage, and internal reporting.
  • Consent — optional cookies and any processing presented as consent-based.
  • Legal obligation — retention and disclosure where the law requires it.

4. How Ukti shares information

Ukti discloses information to the following categories of recipients:

  • Cloud and authentication providers. Ukti uses infrastructure providers for authentication, databases, file storage, and hosting in the regions configured for production.
  • AI model providers. Ukti sends prompts, images, and related context to model providers to generate styling outputs. Those providers process data under their terms and Ukti's instructions as processors where applicable.
  • Operational telemetry. On ukti.io, Ukti loads first-party Vercel Speed Insights to measure page performance (LCP, CLS, TTFB). No behavioral analytics, no tracking pixels, no third-party advertising scripts run on this site.
  • Payment processors. If paid features are offered in future, payment details are collected by the processor; Ukti receives limited billing metadata.
  • Professional advisers and corporate transactions. Lawyers, auditors, insurers, and successors in a merger or acquisition, subject to confidentiality and legal requirements.
  • Authorities. When Ukti believes in good faith that disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, or security.

Ukti does not sell your personal information as defined under U.S. state privacy laws, and Ukti does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5. Data retention

  • Account and profile data are kept while your account is active and for up to seven years after closure where tax, fraud, or legal hold requirements apply.
  • Photos, wardrobe items, and styling content are retained until you delete them or delete your account, after which they are removed from active systems; backup copies typically expire within thirty-five days.
  • Logs and telemetry are retained for up to twelve months, except longer retention for security investigations or legal obligations.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing, to withdraw consent, to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise rights, email privacy@ukti.io. Ukti verifies requests by confirming control of your account email where appropriate.

If you are in India, you may also contact the grievance channel at the same email with "Grievance" in the subject line for matters under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, including access, correction, erasure, consent withdrawal, and nomination rights where applicable.

7. Account and data deletion

You can delete individual photos or wardrobe entries inside the app where those controls exist. To delete your entire account, use the in-app account or security settings if available, or email privacy@ukti.io from your registered address with "Account Deletion" in the subject line. Ukti acknowledges deletion requests within three business days and completes deletion of data not subject to a legal retention exception within thirty days.

8. Cookies and tracking

On ukti.io, Ukti uses at most one functional cookie: the Vercel Speed Insights identifier (strictly-necessary, 15-minute TTL, GDPR Article 5(3) Recital 66 exempt). No marketing cookies, no behavioral tracking, no session recorders. You can control cookies through your browser settings; blocking strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the Ukti app, which uses them for sign-in.

9. Security

Ukti uses encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest where supported by providers, access controls, and monitoring designed to protect personal data. No system is perfectly secure; use a strong password and protect your device.

10. International transfers

Ukti operates from India and uses service providers in India, the United States, the European Union, and other regions. When Ukti transfers personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, it uses appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms where required.

11. Children

The services are not directed at children under 13, and Ukti does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Ukti terms require users to be at least 18 in any event. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact privacy@ukti.io and it will be deleted promptly.

12. Automated processing

The Ukti app's daily call is generated automatically and may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a given context. You should not rely on automated outputs alone for decisions with legal, medical, financial, or safety implications. Ukti does not use your content to train third-party foundation models except where a feature explicitly states otherwise and you opt in.

13. United States (California and other states)

California residents may request the rights described in Section 6 and may ask once per calendar year about certain disclosures for direct marketing purposes. Ukti does not discriminate for exercising privacy rights. Authorized agents may submit requests where state law allows.

14. Changes to this Policy

Ukti updates this Policy when its practices change. The new version is posted on this page and the effective date is updated. For material changes, Ukti provides notice by email or in-app message where the law requires.

15. Contact

Questions about this Policy: privacy@ukti.io
Security issues: privacy@ukti.io (subject: "Security")
