2. What this policy covers

This policy covers the UKTI mobile application (iOS + Android) and the UKTI website at ukti.io. It applies to everyone who uses the app or the site, with region-specific provisions called out where they diverge.

3. What we collect

We collect only what the product needs to deliver one outfit verdict a day.

3.1 Account data

  • Email address (for sign-in, account recovery, transactional email).
  • Display name you choose (optional).
  • Authentication tokens issued by our auth provider (Supabase).
  • Subscription status (Apple / Google IAP receipts; we do not see your card number).

3.2 Photos you upload

  • Wardrobe photos (garments you already own, to build the closet the verdict draws from).
  • A selfie (used for skin analysis and tone / undertone reading).
  • A body photo (used for proportion and fit reading).

We store photos in Supabase storage buckets, encrypted at rest. We serve them to the app through short-lived signed URLs only. We do not embed permanent public image URLs anywhere.

3.3 Wardrobe metadata

We derive this from your wardrobe photos by computer-vision analysis: item name, category, color, brand (if visible), season, and occasion tags. You can edit or delete any of this.

3.4 Skin-analysis outputs

We derive these from your selfie: skin type, tone, undertone, simple concern flags (for example dryness, dehydration), and a short editorial summary. These are stored alongside the photo and are yours to delete.

This is styling, not medicine. The skin analysis is editorial guidance about color, tone, and ritual — it is not a medical diagnosis, not medical advice, and not a replacement for a qualified dermatologist or physician. If you have a medical concern about your skin or health, see a qualified professional.

3.5 Usage telemetry

We record only the eight product KPIs listed in our internal metric firewall. No engagement metrics. No session-length optimization. No interest profiling.

3.6 Device metadata

  • Device model, OS version, app version (for crash reports and compatibility).
  • Locale and timezone (to set the notification hour and currency for incidental local display).
  • Push-notification token, if you opt in to the daily whisper.

3.7 What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your contacts.
  • We do not collect your location beyond locale + timezone.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA / AAID).
  • We do not collect audio, microphone, or accelerometer data.

4. How we use what we collect

4.1 To deliver the product

  • Generate one daily outfit verdict from garments you already own.
  • Analyze your skin and surface a weekly ritual protocol.
  • Send the daily notification at the time you set.

4.2 To keep the product working

  • Authenticate your session.
  • Troubleshoot crashes and bugs (aggregate logs only).
  • Honor your subscription entitlements.

4.3 What we do NOT do with your data

  • We do not sell your data. Not to advertisers. Not to retailers. Not to data brokers. Not to anyone.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or retailers. UKTI has no commerce layer. No affiliate links. No co-branded partnerships that would involve user-data sharing.
  • We do not profile you for advertising. There is no ad network integration in UKTI.
  • We do not train third-party AI models on your photos or content. User photos and wardrobe data are excluded from OpenAI / Azure OpenAI / Anthropic model training under our vendor terms (see §7).
  • We do not use your data to build a "taste profile" you can download, share, or be evaluated against. Personalization is not a slider.

4.4 Legal bases (GDPR Art. 6 / UK GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — delivering the product you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — debugging, security, fraud prevention. We have assessed this balancing test; the record is available on request.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — push notifications; optional skin analysis if it qualifies as special-category data (see §3.4).
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — tax records, regulator responses.

5. How long we keep it

  • Account data — life of account + 30 days after deletion request.
  • Photos (wardrobe, selfie, body) — until you delete the item, or 30 days after account deletion.
  • Wardrobe metadata — same as photos.
  • Skin-analysis outputs — until you delete the report, or 30 days after account deletion.
  • Usage telemetry (the eight KPIs) — 24 months, aggregated-only after 90 days.
  • Subscription / billing records — 7 years (tax obligation).
  • Server logs (crash, security) — 90 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures: encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), encryption at rest (provider-managed), row-level security on the database, short-lived signed URLs for image access, and principle-of-least-privilege access controls.

No system is perfect. If we learn of a breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant regulator within the timelines each regulation requires (GDPR: 72 hours to the regulator; CCPA: without unreasonable delay; DPDP: as soon as possible per §8(6)).
